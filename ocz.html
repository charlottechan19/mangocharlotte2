Ontario Celebration Zone

Celebrating the 2015 Pan Am / Parapan Am Games in Toronto View All Projects  ·  Previous / Next

Description

This summer, two giant inflatable tents were hosted by the Harbourfront Center in celebration of the Pan American Games/ Para Pan American Games. Featuring a 400ft zipine, chef demonstrations, and 38 days of free entertainment and concerts, thousands of visitors came to the pavillion.Mango Charlotte was responsible for event management and overseeing staff. Additionally, design work such as menus, banners, and posters were designed and printed.
